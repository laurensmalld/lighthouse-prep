Vegan Mac n Cheez

a quick n tasty meal in minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 oz. of uncooked pasta (macaroni is the traditional choice but you do you here)
  • 3 tablespoons oil (or vegan butter)
  • 3 tablespoons of your favourite non-dairy milk (I use almond)
  • 1/4 cup vegan cheez sauce mix (get the instructions for how to make your own here)

How to make it

Directions:

  1. Cook your pasta according to package directions. While it's cooking, get the rest of the ingredients ready -- this is a quick dish to make so the faster you go, the sooner you can be eating!
  2. Drain water from pasta and leave the pasta in the strainer for a couple minutes while you whisk the sauce ingredients together in the warm pot.
  3. Once the oil/butter is melted and the cheez sauce mix has been fully mixed in, add the cooked pasta back to the pot.
  4. Stir to completely coat the pasta with sauce.
  5. Add a little salt and pepper to taste and voila -- you've got a vegan dinner!
